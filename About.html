About

This application is made so that you can see how much time you spend on productive web pages to program.

The inspiration

This idea arose because we as software developers often tend to procrastinate with web applications that are just distractions and also distract us with our co-workers causing less productivity in our working hours We wanted to create this chrome extension so that we and our co-workers can compete for better productivity at work though a game.
